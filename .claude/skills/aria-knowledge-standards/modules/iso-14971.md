# ISO 14971:2019 - Risk Management

## Overview

ISO 14971:2019은 의료기기 위험 관리 (Risk Management)에 대한 국제 표준입니다. 의료기기의 전체 수명주기 동안 위험을 식별, 평가, 통제, 모니터링하는 프로세스를 제공합니다.

## Key Changes from 2012 Version

1. **Benefit-risk balance 강조**: Benefit-risk ratio 명시적 요구
2. **Risk-benefit trade-off 개선**: 방향성 제공
3. **Risk management file 강화**: Documentation 요구사항 강화
4. **Post-production monitoring 강조**: PMS 데이터 활용
5. **Information for safety**: Information for safety의 역할 명확화

## Risk Management Principles

1. **Risk는 모든 의료기기에 내재**: Risk는 완전히 제거할 수 없음
2. **Risk는 최대한 감소 (AFAP)**: As Far As Possible
3. **Residual risk는 수용 가능해야 함**: Residual risk acceptability
4. **Risk management는 연속적**: 전체 수명주기 동안 지속

## Risk Definitions (Critical)

- **Hazard (위험 요소)**: 잠재적 해악의 원천
- **Harm (해악)**: 신체적 부상 또는 건강 손상
- **Hazardous Situation (위험 상황)**: 위험 요소에 노출되는 상황
- **Risk (위험)**: 해악 발생 확률과 심각도의 조합
- **Risk Acceptability (위험 수용 가능성)**:
  - **Acceptable**: 추가 감소가 실제 불가능
  - **ALARP**: As Low As Reasonably Practicable (합리적으로 실현 가능한 한 최저)
  - **Unacceptable**: 반드시 감소 필요

## Risk Management Process (Clause 4-9)

### Clause 4: Risk Analysis

**Purpose**: Identify and estimate risks

#### 4.1 Risk Analysis Process

**Step 1: Intended Use and Foreseeable Misuse**
- 의도된 사용 (Intended use)
- 예측 가능한 오용 (Foreseeable misuse)
- 사용자 그룹 식별
- 사용 환경 고려

**Step 2: Characteristics Related to Safety**
- 의료기기의 안전 관련 특성 파악
- 구성 요소, 부속품, 소프트웨어
- 사용 환경, 운영 조건
- 유지 보수, 폐기

**Step 3: Hazard Identification**
- Hazard Identification Methods:
  - Brainstorming
  - Literature review
  - Standards review (Annex C: Hazard scenarios)
  - FMEA (Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)
  - FTA (Fault Tree Analysis)
  - HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points)
  - SWIFT (Structured What-If Technique)

- **Common Hazard Categories**:
  - **Energy hazards**: 전기, 열, 방사선, 기계적 에너지
  - **Biological hazards**: 생물학적 오염, 독성, 감염
  - **Environmental hazards**: 온도, 습도, 진동, 먼지
  - **Functional failures**: 고장, 오작동, 오차
  - **Use errors**: 사용자 실수, 교육 부족
  - **Information hazards**: 경고 부족, 라벨링 오류

**Step 4: Risk Estimation**

For each hazardous situation, estimate:
- **Severity (심각도)**: Harm의 심각도
- **Probability (발생 확률)**: Harm 발생 확률

**Severity Scales**:

| Level | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| 5 | Catastrophic | Death, permanent disability |
| 4 | Serious | Severe injury, permanent impairment |
| 3 | Moderate | Minor injury, temporary impairment |
| 2 | Minor | First aid, temporary discomfort |
| 1 | Negligible | Inconvenience, no injury |

**Probability Scales**:

| Level | Description | Frequency |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 5 | Frequent | > 10% of uses |
| 4 | Probable | 1-10% of uses |
| 3 | Occasional | 0.1-1% of uses |
| 2 | Remote | 0.01-0.1% of uses |
| 1 | Improbable | < 0.01% of uses |

**Risk Index**:
```
Risk Index = Severity × Probability
```

### Clause 5: Risk Evaluation

**Purpose**: Compare estimated risks against acceptability criteria

**Risk Matrix**:

| Severity \ Probability | Improbable (1) | Remote (2) | Occasional (3) | Probable (4) | Frequent (5) |
|----------------------|----------------|------------|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| Catastrophic (5) | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 | 25 |
| Serious (4) | 4 | 8 | 12 | 16 | 20 |
| Moderate (3) | 3 | 6 | 9 | 12 | 15 |
| Minor (2) | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 |
| Negligible (1) | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |

**Acceptability Criteria**:

| Risk Index | Acceptability | Action |
|------------|---------------|--------|
| 1-3 | Acceptable | Routine monitoring |
| 4-8 | Acceptable with review | Periodic review |
| 9-15 | ALARP | Mitigation required |
| 16-25 | Unacceptable | Immediate mitigation |

### Clause 6: Risk Control

**Purpose**: Reduce risks to acceptable levels

#### 6.1 Risk Control Options (in order of preference)

**Option 1: Inherent Safety by Design**
- Hazard를 설계에서 제거
- 예: 전원을 낮은 전압으로 변경, 천연 고무 제거

**Option 2: Protective Measures**
- 안전 메커니즘 추가
- 예: 과전류 보호, 폐색 방지, 감시 알림

**Option 3: Information for Safety**
- 경고, 주의사항, 금기사항
- 예: 사용자 매뉴얼, 라벨링, 교육 프로그램

#### 6.2 Implementation of Risk Control Measures

- Risk control 조치의 효과성 검증
- 새로운 위험 도입 여부 확인
- Benefit-risk ratio 재평가

#### 6.3 Residual Risk Assessment

- Residual risk (잔여 위험) 평가
- Residual risk acceptability 확인
- Risk-benefit 분석

### Clause 7: Overall Residual Risk Evaluation

**Purpose**: Aggregate all residual risks and evaluate overall acceptability

- 모든 residual risk를 집계
- 전체 residual risk acceptability 평가
- Risk-benefit 분석 수행
- Risk-benefit ratio가 환자에게 유리한지 확인

### Clause 8: Risk Management Review

**Purpose**: Periodic review of risk management process

- 정기적 risk management review
- 새로운 정보 반영
- Experience feedback 반영
- Risk management file 갱신

### Clause 9: Production and Post-production Activities

**Purpose**: Monitor risks throughout product lifecycle

#### 9.1 Post-Production Monitoring

**Monitoring Sources**:
- Complaints
- Adverse event reports
- Field safety corrective actions (FSCA)
- Scientific literature
- User feedback

**Trigger Events for Review**:
- New safety information
- Change in intended use
- Manufacturing process changes
- Regulatory changes

#### 9.2 Risk Management Report

- Post-production monitoring 데이터 분석
- Emerging risks 식별
- Corrective actions 필요 시 결정
- Risk management file 업데이트

## Risk Management File (RMF)

**Required Contents**:

1. **Risk Management Plan**
   - Scope, activities, responsibilities
   - Criteria for risk acceptability
   - Verification activities

2. **Risk Analysis**
   - Hazard identification
   - Risk estimation

3. **Risk Evaluation**
   - Risk acceptability determination

4. **Risk Control**
   - Risk control measures
   - Effectiveness verification

5. **Residual Risk Assessment**
   - Residual risk acceptability
   - Benefit-risk analysis

6. **Risk Management Report**
   - Review results
   - Conclusion

## Risk Analysis Tools

### FMEA (Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)

**Purpose**: Bottom-up analysis of component failures

**Steps**:
1. Component functions 식별
2. Failure modes 식별
3. Effects 분석
4. Severity, Occurrence, Detection 평가
5. RPN (Risk Priority Number) 계산

**RPN = Severity × Occurrence × Detection**

**Advantages**:
- Component-level analysis
- Quantitative approach
- Design & process FMEA

**Limitations**:
- Time-consuming
- Single failure focus
- Human error dependency

### FTA (Fault Tree Analysis)

**Purpose**: Top-down analysis of system failures

**Steps**:
1. Undesirable event 정의
2. Fault tree 구성
3. Logical gates (AND, OR) 적용
4. Minimal cut sets 식별
5. Probability 계산

**Advantages**:
- System-level perspective
- Multiple failure modes
- Probability analysis

**Limitations**:
- Complex to construct
- Requires expertise
- Binary logic limitation

### HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points)

**Purpose**: Process-oriented hazard control

**7 Principles**:
1. Hazard analysis
2. Critical control points (CCPs) 식별
3. Critical limits 설정
4. Monitoring procedures
5. Corrective actions
6. Verification procedures
7. Record-keeping procedures

**Advantages**:
- Process-focused
- Prevention-oriented
- Regulatory recognition

## Common Deficiencies

1. **Hazard identification incomplete**: Missing hazard categories
2. **Risk estimation qualitative only**: No numerical values
3. **Risk control options not evaluated**: Jumping to information for safety
4. **Residual risk not assessed**: No residual risk evaluation
5. **Benefit-risk analysis missing**: No benefit consideration
6. **RMF incomplete**: Missing required sections
7. **Post-production monitoring absent**: No PMS integration
8. **Risk acceptability criteria undefined**: No clear thresholds
9. **Risk control verification missing**: No effectiveness demonstration
10. **Overall residual risk not evaluated**: No aggregation of risks
