# Research Resources Reference

Curated list of authoritative regulatory research sources and databases for medical device RA/QA professionals.

## Regulatory Authority Databases

### FDA (United States)

**FDA.gov Main Portal**
- URL: https://www.fda.gov
- Content: Regulations, guidance documents, device approvals
- Search: https://www.fda.gov/search

**21 CFR (Code of Federal Regulations)**
- URL: https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-21
- Content: Current FDA regulations
- Update Frequency: Daily

**FDA 510(k) Database**
- URL: https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfpmn/pmn.cfm
- Content: 510(k) clearances, summaries, statements
- Search: By device name, 510(k) number, product code, applicant

**FDA PMA Database**
- URL: https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfpma/pma.cfm
- Content: PMA approvals, supplements, SSED
- Search: By device name, PMA number, applicant

**FDA Guidance Documents**
- URL: https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/device-advice-comprehensive-regulatory-assistance/guidance-documents-medical-devices-and-radiation-emitting-products
- Content: Final and draft guidance documents
- Organization: By topic and device type

**Federal Register**
- URL: https://www.federalregister.gov
- Content: Proposed rules, final rules, notices
- Search: Medical device notices and regulatory changes

### EU (European Union)

**EU MDR/IVDR Official Text**
- URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu
- Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (MDR): https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2017/745
- Regulation (EU) 2017/746 (IVDR): https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2017/746
- Content: Official regulatory text in all EU languages

**EUDAMED (EU Database on Medical Devices)**
- URL: https://ec.europa.eu/tools/eudamed
- Content: Device registrations, certificates, UDI database
- Access: Public modules available

**MDCG Guidance Documents**
- URL: https://health.ec.europa.eu/medical-devices-topics-interest/new-regulations/guidance-mdcg-endorsed-documents-and-other-guidance_en
- Content: MDCG guidance documents for MDR/IVDR
- Organization: By topic and regulation

**Official Journal of the EU**
- URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/oj/direct-access.html
- Content: Harmonized standards, implementing regulations
- Search: Medical device related publications

### MFDS (Korea)

**MFDS Main Portal**
- URL: https://www.mfds.go.kr
- Content: Korean regulations, guidance, approvals
- Language: Korean (some English content available)

**Medical Device Information**
- URL: https://www.mfds.go.kr/eng/wpge/m_18/de010601l001.do
- Content: Medical device regulatory information
- Language: English version available

## Clinical Literature Databases

### PubMed/MEDLINE

**PubMed Portal**
- URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov
- Content: Biomedical literature database
- Coverage: 35+ million citations
- Access: Free public access

**Search Tips:**
- Use MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) terms
- Filter by publication date, article type
- Use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT)

### Cochrane Library

**Cochrane Portal**
- URL: https://www.cochranelibrary.com
- Content: Systematic reviews, clinical trials
- Focus: Evidence-based medicine
- Access: Subscription required (some free access)

### ClinicalTrials.gov

**ClinicalTrials.gov Portal**
- URL: https://clinicaltrials.gov
- Content: Clinical trial registry
- Search: By condition, intervention, device
- Access: Free public access

## Industry Guidance Organizations

### AAMI (Association for the Advancement of Medical Instrumentation)

**AAMI Main Portal**
- URL: https://www.aami.org
- Content: Standards, guidance documents, educational resources
- Access: Membership required for full access

**Key AAMI Standards:**
- ANSI/AAMI TIR45 (Guidance for medical device cybersecurity)
- ANSI/AAMI SW96 (Medical device software risk management)

### GHTF (Global Harmonization Task Force)

**GHTF Documents Archive**
- URL: https://www.imdrf.org/ghtf-documents
- Content: Historical harmonization guidance
- Status: Archived (replaced by IMDRF)

**Note:** GHTF disbanded in 2012, replaced by IMDRF (International Medical Device Regulators Forum)

### IMDRF (International Medical Device Regulators Forum)

**IMDRF Main Portal**
- URL: https://www.imdrf.org
- Content: Harmonization guidance, working group documents
- Topics: Software, cybersecurity, UDI, clinical evaluation

```
1. Resolve library identifier:
   mcp__context7__resolve-library-id: "ISO 13485"

   mcp__context7__get-library-docs: library_id, section_filter
```

## Quality Verification Checklist

Before using any resource:

- [ ] Verify source authority (official regulatory body or recognized organization)
- [ ] Check currency (current effective version, not superseded)
- [ ] Confirm applicability (relevant to device type and target market)
- [ ] Ensure traceability (verifiable URL, document number, access method)
- [ ] Use Context7 MCP for standards to ensure current version
- [ ] Document source quality assessment score (see quality-assessment.md)
