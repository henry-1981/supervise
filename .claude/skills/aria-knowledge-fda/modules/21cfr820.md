# FDA 21 CFR 820 - Quality System Regulation (QSR)

## Overview

21 CFR 820 establishes requirements for quality systems for medical devices. It is the foundational regulation for all FDA-regulated medical devices.

## Key Subparts

### Subpart B - Quality System Requirements (820.20)

**Management Responsibility (820.20)**
- Quality policy
- Quality objectives
- Management with executive responsibility
- Organizational structure
- Management review

### Subpart C - Design Controls (820.30)

**Design and Development Procedures**

**820.30(a) Design and Development Planning**
- Define design and development activities
- Assign responsibility
- Include interfaces between different groups
- Update plans as design evolves

**8.20.30(b) Design Input**
- Establish design requirements
- Ensure appropriate and complete
- Address intended use, user needs, safety
- Document design inputs

**820.30(c) Design Output**
- Document design outputs in approved form
- Translate design inputs into design outputs
- Include acceptance criteria
- Identify essential design outputs

**820.30(d) Design Review**
- Conduct formal design reviews
- Document design reviews
- Include independent reviewer(s)
- Review at appropriate stages

**820.30(e) Design Verification**
- Verify design output meets design input
- Document verification activities
- Confirm acceptance criteria are met

**820.30(f) Design Validation**
- Validate device design for intended use
- Includes software validation and risk analysis
- Document validation activities
- Ensure device meets user needs

**820.30(g) Design Transfer**
- Transfer design to production specifications
- Document design transfer
- Ensure translation of design output correctly

**820.30(h) Design Changes**
- Identify, document, verify, validate design changes
- Approve design changes before implementation
- Maintain design history file (DHF)

**820.30(i) Design History File**
- Compile DHF for each device
- Document design history
- Demonstrate design was developed in accordance with approved design plan

### Subpart H - Acceptance Activities (820.80)

**820.80 Receiving, In-Process, and Finished Device Acceptance**

- **Receiving Acceptance**: Ensure purchased items meet specifications
- **In-Process Acceptance**: Control in-process product
- **Finished Device Acceptance**: Final acceptance activities
- **Acceptance Records**: Document acceptance activities

### Subpart I - Nonconforming Product (820.90)

**820.90 Nonconforming Product**

- Identify nonconforming product
- Control nonconforming product
- Review nonconformity
- Document nonconformity and disposition

### Subpart J - Corrective and Preventive Action (820.100)

**820.100 Corrective and Preventive Action**

- Analyze processes, work operations, quality problems
- Investigate causes of nonconforming product
- Identify actions needed to prevent recurrence
- Implement and record changes
- Verify effectiveness of actions
- Document CAPA activities

## Essential Terms

- **DHF (Design History File)**: Compilation of design records
- **DMR (Device Master Record)**: Compilation of procedures and specifications
- **DHR (Device History Record)**: Compilation of production records
- **CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action)**: Quality improvement process
