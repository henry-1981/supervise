# Standards Overview

## Standards in Medical Device Regulation

Standards provide technical specifications to demonstrate compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Standards Hierarchy

```
1. Statutory Requirements (Laws, Regulations)
2. Mandatory Standards (Referenced by law)
3. Voluntary Standards (Consensus standards)
4. Manufacturer Standards (Internal procedures)
```

## ISO 13485: Quality Management System

### Key Clauses

**Clause 4: Quality Management System**
- 4.1 General requirements
- 4.2 Documentation requirements

**Clause 7: Product Realization**
- 7.3 Design and development
- 7.5 Production and service provision

**Clause 8: Measurement, Analysis and Improvement**
- 8.2 Monitoring and measurement
- 8.3 Control of nonconforming product

## ISO 14971: Risk Management

### Risk Management Process

**Clause 4: Risk Analysis**
- 4.1 Risk analysis process
- 4.2 Intended use and foreseeable misuse
- 4.3 Identification of hazards
- 4.4 Estimation of risks

**Clause 5: Risk Evaluation**
- 5.1 Risk evaluation process
- 5.2 Risk acceptability criteria

**Clause 6: Risk Control**
- 6.1 Risk control options analysis
- 6.2 Implementation of risk control measures

## IEC 62304: Medical Device Software

### Software Safety Classification

**Class A (No injury)**
- No special requirements beyond QMS

**Class B (Non-serious injury)**
- Software requirements specification
- Detailed design
- Unit testing and integration testing

**Class C (Death or serious injury)**
- All Class B requirements plus:
- Verification of risk control measures
- Traceability analysis
