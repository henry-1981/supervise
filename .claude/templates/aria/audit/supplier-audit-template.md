## Template Usage Notes

1. **Customization Instructions**:
   - Add supplier-specific requirements
   - Adjust for product/service type
   - Modify risk criteria to match organization standards

2. **Preparation**:
   - Complete pre-audit questionnaire
   - Review supplier performance data
   - Prepare audit agenda

3. **Conducting the Audit**:
   - Verify processes, not just documents
   - Interview personnel at all levels
   - Observe actual operations

4. **Follow-up**:
   - Track supplier corrective actions
   - Verify effectiveness of corrections
   - Schedule follow-up audit if needed
