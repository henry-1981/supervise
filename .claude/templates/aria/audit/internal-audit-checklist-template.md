## Template Usage Notes

1. **Customization Instructions**:
   - Add organization-specific questions
   - Include additional processes as needed
   - Adjust checklist for audit scope

2. **Audit Planning**:
   - Use checklist to plan audit activities
   - Allocate time based on process complexity
   - Identify evidence sources in advance

3. **Conducting the Audit**:
   - Ask open-ended questions
   - Verify statements with evidence
   - Document all findings objectively

4. **Follow-up**:
   - Track corrective actions to completion
   - Verify effectiveness of corrections
   - Schedule follow-up audit if needed
